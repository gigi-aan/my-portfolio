    CECE's Online Ordering Website

    Role

    Full Stack Developer

    Duration

    August 2024 - March 2025

    Team

    4 Members

    Tech Stack

    HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, SQL

    Overview

    As part of an academic project, I developed and deployed a full-stack online ordering website for Cece’s, a simulated case based on a small local food business. I handled various roles from backend to frontend development, as well as the UI design, to create a seamless digital ordering experience.

    Problem

    Cece’s relied entirely on face-to-face transactions and third-party platforms like Grab and Foodpanda, which limited direct order management, reduced profit margins, and created dependence on external systems. Our goal was to design a standalone ordering platform the business could fully control.

    Outcome

    My team and I built a fully functional ordering system using PHP, MySQL, HTML, and CSS that lets users view the menu, place orders, and receive confirmations. The project was successfully presented and received strong feedback from faculty panelists for its real-world relevance and technical execution.
